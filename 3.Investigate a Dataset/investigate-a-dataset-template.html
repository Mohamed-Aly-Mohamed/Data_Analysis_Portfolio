Introduction¶

This data set contains information about 10,000 movies collected from The Movies Database (TMDb), including user ratings and revenue. In this project we will show how the different factors (independent factors) affect the revenue (dependent factor) of different movies. Also there is some questions to investigate this dataset for like : what are the highest revenue movies according to duration? ,what are the sum of revenues against budgets over years? , what are the highest revenue movies according to vote average review? ,what are the highest revenue movies according to genres? . All of these questions will be answered in the following investigation

Conclusions¶

In all our analysis , we deal with revenue as our dependent variable while other variables are independent like movie runtime , vote average review , movie budget and movie genres. Also there are some limitations on the main dataset , as there are some missing values and some nulls that i eliminate(drop) during my processing.

Summary of Analysis:¶

  1. From the first question (Q1 : what are the highest revenue movies according to duration?) , we found that long movies revenue are much higher than moderate or short runtime movies.
  2. From the second question (Q2 what are the sum of revenues against budgets over years?) , we found that as the budget of the movie increases , the revenue of the movie also increases , they are directly proportional to each other , which indicates strong correlation coefficient.
  3. From the third question (Q3 : what are the highest revenue movies according to vote average review?) , we found that as the average vote of the movie increases , the revenue of the movie also increases , they are directly proportional to each other , which indicates strong correlation coefficient , evenif the number of produced high vote movies are lower than low vote movies.
  4. From the fourth question (Q4 : what are the highest revenue movies according to genres?) , we found that the top three movies production categories are Drama , Comedy and Action.Unexpected High revenues for Animation movies evenif the number of produced animation movies is very low compared to the action or drama movies.
